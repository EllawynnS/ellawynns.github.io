01 — About

Orbiting multiple
disciplines at once.

I'm a Computer Engineering student currently considering a pivot into Geological Engineering — because rocks and circuits have more in common than you'd think. Both are systems that follow rules, break in interesting ways, and reward careful observation.

My academic path has led me toward robotics and embedded systems, where I love the challenge of making physical hardware respond intelligently to the world. I'm equally drawn to GIS, mapping, and visual statistics — translating complex spatial data into something humans can actually understand.

When I'm not at a desk, I'm outdoors — hiking, reading terrain, watching for wildlife. That connection to the physical world is what originally sparked my passion for planetary science and astronomy: if I love Earth this much, imagine the others.
